🥺 A Proper Apology

Have you ever found yourself in a position where you needed to apologize to someone but couldn’t think of the right words or the right sentence?

Well, if you ask me the answer is going to be “Plenty of times”.

I have the ultimate power to make people go mad at me and often times I don’t really apologize. >> No! I’m not a narcissist, in case you’re wondering.

Well, to be honest, Sometimes I apologize way too much - I mean WAY TOO MUCH and sometimes I don't do it.

But if Sheldon has taught me something - it is how to make a proper apology. Therefore, When I do ask for forgiveness, I try keeping up with the points.

It is quite a Mantra.

So, an apology requires three prime steps.

Step 1. An admission of Wrongdoing.

Step 2. A promise never to repeat said action.

Step 3. An earnest request for forgiveness.

So, next time when you apologize – Do keep these steps handy. 😊
